# Getting Started & Common How-Tos

Sample knowledge-base document for the RAG index (see `data/kb/`).

## Changing your plan
Go to **Settings → Billing → Plan** and choose Free, Pro, or Enterprise. Upgrades
take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

## Exporting data
Use **File → Export**. PDF export is supported on v3.3+. If export crashes on
older versions, upgrade to the latest release.

## Resetting your password
Use **Forgot password** on the login screen; a reset link is emailed to you.

## Contacting support
Email support@example.com or open a ticket in-app. Include your account email and
a description of the issue.
