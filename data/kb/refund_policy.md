# Refund Policy

This is a sample knowledge-base document used to seed the RAG index. Member B's
`rag/ingest.py` chunks + embeds files in `data/kb/` at startup.

## Eligibility
- Duplicate charges are always refundable in full.
- Pro/Enterprise plans can be refunded within 14 days of the charge if unused.
- Free plans have no charges and are not refundable.

## Process
1. Confirm the charge in the customer's order history.
2. Refunds above $100 require human approval (escalation).
3. Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

## Cancellations
- Customers can cancel any time; access continues until the end of the billing period.
- Cancellation does not automatically issue a refund.
